About Us:

Seafood Souq (SFS) intends to lead the B2B / B2C e-commerce movement in the region for the seafood industry by providing a platform allowing suppliers to sell products directly to the buyers. SFS provides the structure for safe and quick payment for sellers, enabling highly competitive pricing, product traceability, and value-added services including credit terms, customs clearing, inspection and delivery.

Seafood Souq platform is owned and operated by Seafood Souq General Trading LLC.
